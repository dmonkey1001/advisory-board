NASCIO 2025: Top Priorities for State CIOs

#1
Cybersecurity & Risk Management

For the 12th consecutive year, cybersecurity remains the undisputed top priority, evolving from a technical issue to a core pillar of government stability and public trust.

The strategic landscape is defined by a clear hierarchy of challenges. While cybersecurity leads, AI has surged to the forefront, creating a complex web of dependencies with foundational pillars like modernization and budget control.
